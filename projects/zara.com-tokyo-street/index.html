Miguel Vides is an Art Director focused on Graphic Design and Photography based in Madrid. He has worked across cultural, music, and fashion fields, Miguel Vides is an Art Director focused on Graphic Design and Photography based in Madrid. He has worked across cultural, music, and fashion fields, collaborating with clients such as ICON El País, Zara, Carolina Durante, Zeit Magazine, La Pija y la Quinqui, among others.

His work has been published in It’s Nice That, Acero Magazine, and Drool Art. He was also a finalist in the 2026 Miquel Casablancas Award.
